# Treaty of Tordesillas Signed

![Treaty of Tordesillas Signed](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bc/Treaty_of_Tordesillas.jpg)

## 1494-06-07

On June 7, 1494, Spain and Portugal formally signed the Treaty of Tordesillas in Tordesillas, Spain. This landmark agreement aimed to divide the newly discovered lands outside Europe between the two burgeoning colonial powers. The treaty drew an imaginary line 370 leagues west of the Cape Verde Islands, granting Portugal dominion over lands to the east of the line and Spain control over lands to the west. This effectively gave Portugal control of Brazil and its eastern trade routes, while Spain received the vast majority of the Americas. The treaty profoundly shaped the colonial histories and linguistic maps of South America and other global regions.
