# The 20 July Plot to Assassinate Hitler

![The 20 July Plot to Assassinate Hitler](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/77/Bundesarchiv_Bild_146-1972-025-10%2C_Hitler-Attentat%2C_20._Juli_1944.jpg)

## 1944-07-20

On July 20, 1944, a group of German officers, led by Colonel Claus von Stauffenberg, attempted to assassinate Adolf Hitler at his "Wolf's Lair" headquarters near Rastenburg, East Prussia. Stauffenberg placed a bomb in a briefcase under a conference table during a meeting with Hitler and other high-ranking officials. The bomb detonated, but Hitler survived with only minor injuries, largely due to a heavy oak table leg shielding him from the full force of the blast. This failed assassination attempt led to a brutal crackdown by the Nazi regime, resulting in the execution of thousands of suspected conspirators and their families. The plot, known as Operation Valkyrie, represented a significant act of internal German resistance against Hitler's tyranny during World War II.
