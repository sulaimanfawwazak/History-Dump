# King John Seals Magna Carta

![King John Seals Magna Carta](http://blogs.loc.gov/law/files/2014/06/King-John-seal-Obverse-detail.jpg)

## 1215-06-15

On June 15, 1215, King John of England was compelled by a group of rebellious barons to seal the Magna Carta at Runnymede, near Windsor. This historic document, often considered the first step towards a constitutional monarchy, aimed to limit the king's power and protect certain rights of his subjects. It established principles such as the right to a fair trial and that even the monarch was subject to the rule of law. Although initially a failed peace treaty and annulled shortly after, the Magna Carta's foundational concepts were repeatedly reissued and profoundly influenced the development of parliamentary democracy and individual liberties in future centuries, becoming a landmark in legal history.
