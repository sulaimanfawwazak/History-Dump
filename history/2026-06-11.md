# Three Inmates Escape Alcatraz

![Three Inmates Escape Alcatraz](https://e3.365dm.com/16/07/1600x900/alcatraz-escapees-1_3507988.jpg?20160704113948)

## 1962-06-11

On June 11, 1962, Frank Morris, John Anglin, and Clarence Anglin executed a daring escape from Alcatraz Federal Penitentiary, a maximum-security prison located on an island in San Francisco Bay. Over several months, the inmates meticulously planned their breakout, using makeshift tools to widen air vent openings in their cells and constructing crude rafts from stolen raincoats. They left papier-mâché dummy heads in their beds to deceive guards during night checks, buying them crucial time for their escape into the frigid waters. Despite extensive searches by authorities, the three men were never seen again, and their fate remains officially unknown, although they are presumed to have drowned. This infamous event contributed to the permanent closure of Alcatraz less than a year later.
