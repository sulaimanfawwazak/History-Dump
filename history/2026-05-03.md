# Margaret Thatcher Becomes Prime Minister of the United Kingdom

![Margaret Thatcher Becomes Prime Minister of the United Kingdom](https://cdn.britannica.com/54/204154-050-D2C394B3/Margaret-Thatcher-1980.jpg)

## 1979-05-03

On May 3, 1979, Margaret Thatcher was elected Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, becoming the first woman to hold the office. Her victory came after the Conservative Party won a decisive majority in the general election, ousting the Labour government led by James Callaghan. Thatcher's tenure, lasting until 1990, was characterized by her conservative policies, including privatization of state-owned industries and curbs on trade union power, which became known as "Thatcherism." Her leadership profoundly reshaped British politics and society, earning her the nickname "The Iron Lady."
