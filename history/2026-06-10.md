# Italy Declares War On France And The United Kingdom

![Italy Declares War On France And The United Kingdom](https://i.redd.it/5kyekrr5a26f1.jpeg)

## 1940-06-10

On June 10, 1940, during World War II, Benito Mussolini, the Fascist dictator of Italy, formally declared war on France and the United Kingdom. Speaking from the balcony of Palazzo Venezia in Rome, Mussolini announced Italy's entry into the conflict as an ally of Nazi Germany. This pivotal decision came as German forces were decisively conquering France, prompting Italy to seize what it perceived as an opportune moment to expand its territorial influence. Italy's declaration significantly escalated the war, extending the conflict into the Mediterranean Sea and North Africa, where its forces would soon engage Allied powers. The move was met with mixed reactions internationally, further cementing the division of Europe into Axis and Allied blocs.
