# Wilt Chamberlain Scores 100 Points

![Wilt Chamberlain Scores 100 Points](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/65/Wilt_Chamberlain_100_Point_Game_1962_%28original%29.jpg)

## 1962-03-02

On March 2, 1962, legendary basketball player Wilt Chamberlain achieved an unprecedented feat in professional basketball, scoring an astounding 100 points in a single game. Playing for the Philadelphia Warriors against the New York Knicks at Hersheypark Arena in Hershey, Pennsylvania, Chamberlain's performance remains the highest individual scoring effort in NBA history. He shot 36-for-63 from the field and 28-for-32 from the free-throw line, leading the Warriors to a 169-147 victory. This iconic record cemented Chamberlain's status as one of the sport's greatest offensive forces and is frequently cited as one of the most unbreakable records in sports.
