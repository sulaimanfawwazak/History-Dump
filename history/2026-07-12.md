# King Henry VIII Marries Catherine Parr

![King Henry VIII Marries Catherine Parr](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/49/Catherine_Parr_from_NPG.jpg)

## 1543-07-12

On July 12, 1543, King Henry VIII married his sixth and final wife, Catherine Parr, at Hampton Court Palace. A wealthy widow, Catherine was known for her intelligence and Protestant leanings, which would influence her stepchildren, particularly the future Elizabeth I. This marriage marked the end of Henry's tumultuous marital history, which had significantly shaped the religious and political landscape of England. Catherine skillfully navigated the dangers of Henry's court, even managing to reconcile the King with his daughters, Mary and Elizabeth, before his death in 1547. She is notable as the only one of Henry's wives to outlive him.
