# Yuri Gagarin Becomes First Man In Space

![Yuri Gagarin Becomes First Man In Space](https://cdn.mos.cms.futurecdn.net/oPokc4rVY8SB8wT77vzz84.jpg)

## 1961-04-12

On April 12, 1961, Soviet cosmonaut Yuri Gagarin made history by becoming the first human to journey into outer space. Aboard the Vostok 1 spacecraft, Gagarin completed one orbit of Earth, a flight lasting 108 minutes. This groundbreaking achievement marked a pivotal moment in the Space Race between the United States and the Soviet Union. His successful mission opened the door for human spaceflight and cemented his legacy as a global icon.
