# McDonald's Opens Its First Restaurant

![McDonald's Opens Its First Restaurant](https://s7d1.scene7.com/is/image/mcdonalds/History_Brothers_SanBernadino_2COL_1-1:2-column-desktop?resmode=sharp2)

## 1940-05-15

On May 15, 1940, brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald opened their first restaurant in San Bernardino, California. Initially a barbecue drive-in, the establishment later pivoted in 1948 to a simplified menu focused on hamburgers, milkshakes, and french fries, implementing what they called the 'Speedee Service System' to serve customers quickly. This innovative approach laid the groundwork for the modern fast-food industry. Years later, in 1954, milkshake machine salesman Ray Kroc visited the restaurant and was so impressed that he became a franchising agent, eventually buying out the brothers. Kroc then expanded McDonald's into the global chain it is today, fundamentally transforming the restaurant landscape.
