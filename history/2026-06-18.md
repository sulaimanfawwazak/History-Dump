# Battle Of Waterloo

![Battle Of Waterloo](https://cdn.britannica.com/74/64874-050-596B15CF/Battle-of-Waterloo.jpg)

## 1815-06-18

The Battle of Waterloo was fought on June 18, 1815, near Waterloo in present-day Belgium. It marked the final defeat of Napoleon Bonaparte, who had returned from exile to reclaim power in France during the 'Hundred Days'. A coalition of British, Dutch, Belgian, and German forces led by the Duke of Wellington, reinforced by Prussian armies under Marshal Blücher, decisively defeated Napoleon's French army. This monumental battle concluded the Napoleonic Wars, leading to Napoleon's second and final abdication and exile to Saint Helena. The outcome profoundly reshaped the political landscape of Europe for decades to come.
