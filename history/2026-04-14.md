# Abraham Lincoln Assassinated

![Abraham Lincoln Assassinated](https://fords.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/1870-assassination-depiction-3b52661u.jpg)

## 1865-04-14

On April 14, 1865, just five days after Confederate General Robert E. Lee's surrender effectively ended the American Civil War, President Abraham Lincoln was shot by John Wilkes Booth. The assassination occurred while Lincoln was attending a performance of "Our American Cousin" at Ford's Theatre in Washington D.C. Booth, a well-known actor and Confederate sympathizer, shot Lincoln in the back of the head during the play's third act before escaping the theatre. Lincoln was pronounced dead the following morning, making him the first U.S. President to be assassinated, a profound tragedy that plunged the nation into mourning during a crucial period of reconstruction.
