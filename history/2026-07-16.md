# The Trinity Test: First Atomic Bomb Detonated

![The Trinity Test: First Atomic Bomb Detonated](https://www.afnwc.af.mil/portals/88/Trinity1Large.gif)

## 1945-07-16

On July 16, 1945, at 5:29:45 a.m. local time, the United States conducted the Trinity Test, the first successful detonation of an atomic bomb. This monumental event took place in the Jornada del Muerto desert in New Mexico, as the culmination of the top-secret Manhattan Project. The explosion released energy equivalent to approximately 20 kilotons of TNT, creating a massive mushroom cloud and fusing the desert sand into a new mineral called trinitite. This pivotal test demonstrated the unprecedented destructive power of nuclear weapons, profoundly shaping the conclusion of World War II and forever altering the landscape of global warfare and international relations.
