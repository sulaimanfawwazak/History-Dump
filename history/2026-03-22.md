# The Beatles Release Their Debut Album "Please Please Me"

![The Beatles Release Their Debut Album "Please Please Me"](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/2/2e/Please_Please_Me.png)

## 1963-03-22

On March 22, 1963, The Beatles released their debut album, "Please Please Me," in the United Kingdom. Recorded in a single intense day at EMI Studios (now Abbey Road Studios) in London, the album largely featured songs from their live performances, including their first two hit singles, "Love Me Do" and the title track. The album quickly soared to the top of the UK charts, where it stayed for 30 weeks, establishing the band as a major force in British music. Its phenomenal success marked the official beginning of Beatlemania and set the stage for their unprecedented global dominance, fundamentally altering the landscape of popular music.
