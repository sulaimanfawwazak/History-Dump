# Kingdom Of Italy Proclaimed

![Kingdom Of Italy Proclaimed](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/3/33/Proclamation_of_the_Kingdom_of_Italy.jpg/250px-Proclamation_of_the_Kingdom_of_Italy.jpg)

## 1861-03-17

On March 17, 1861, the Kingdom of Italy was officially proclaimed, marking the culmination of the Risorgimento. Victor Emmanuel II, previously King of Sardinia, was crowned as its first king, unifying most of the Italian peninsula under a single monarchy. This historic event took place in Turin, which initially served as the capital of the newly formed state. The proclamation brought together various independent states and regions, creating a modern nation-state after centuries of fragmentation. This act fundamentally reshaped the political landscape of Europe and established a unified Italian identity.
