# Mount St. Helens Erupts

![Mount St. Helens Erupts](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/dc/MSH82_st_helens_plume_from_harrys_ridge_05-19-82.jpg)

## 1980-05-18

On May 18, 1980, Mount St. Helens, a stratovolcano located in Skamania County, Washington, erupted catastrophically. The eruption was the deadliest and most economically destructive volcanic event in U.S. history, claiming 57 lives and causing widespread damage. A massive debris avalanche, triggered by an earthquake, led to a lateral blast that flattened 600 square kilometers of forest and deposited ash across 11 U.S. states and several Canadian provinces. The event dramatically altered the landscape of the surrounding area and became a significant case study in volcanology and ecological recovery.
