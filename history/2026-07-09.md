# Argentina Declares Independence From Spain

![Argentina Declares Independence From Spain](https://cdn.britannica.com/33/190633-050-F3C4E00A/Argentinian-flags-street-souvenirs.jpg)

## 1816-07-09

On July 9, 1816, the Congress of Tucumán formally declared the independence of the United Provinces of South America from the Spanish Crown. This pivotal moment, taking place in the city of San Miguel de Tucumán, marked the culmination of years of revolutionary struggle and aspiration for self-governance following the May Revolution of 1810. Key figures like General José de San Martín played crucial roles in advocating for and securing this declaration. The declaration officially severed the colonial ties with Spain, establishing a sovereign nation that would eventually become the modern Republic of Argentina. This act ignited further conflicts in the region but was a definitive step towards South American liberation.
