# Apple Introduces the Revolutionary iPhone

![Apple Introduces the Revolutionary iPhone](https://duncanhearing.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Everything-We-Know-About-Apples-Clinical-Grade-Hearing-Aid-Feature-1.jpg)

## 2007-01-09

On January 9, 2007, Apple Inc. CEO Steve Jobs unveiled the original iPhone at the Macworld Conference & Expo in San Francisco, California. Jobs famously described the device as a combination of three revolutionary products: an iPod with a widescreen touchscreen, a revolutionary mobile phone, and a breakthrough internet communications device. This introduction marked a pivotal moment in the history of personal technology, setting the stage for the modern smartphone era. The iPhone, with its multi-touch interface and intuitive operating system, profoundly changed how people interacted with technology and consumed media. Its debut spurred widespread innovation across the mobile industry globally.
