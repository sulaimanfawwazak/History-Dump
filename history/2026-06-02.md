# Coronation Of Queen Elizabeth II

![Coronation Of Queen Elizabeth II](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7c/Queen_Elizabeth_II_on_her_Coronation_Day.jpg)

## 1953-06-02

On June 2, 1953, Elizabeth Alexandra Mary was officially crowned Queen Elizabeth II at Westminster Abbey in London. The elaborate ceremony was historic as it was the first British coronation to be fully televised, a groundbreaking decision that brought the monarchy directly into millions of homes across the United Kingdom and beyond. An estimated 27 million people in the UK alone watched the event, while millions more listened via radio worldwide. This grand spectacle marked the formal beginning of her reign, which would ultimately become the longest of any British monarch in history, spanning over 70 years. The event was a significant moment for both the monarchy and the developing medium of television.
