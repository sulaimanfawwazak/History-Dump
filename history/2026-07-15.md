# Crusaders Capture Jerusalem

![Crusaders Capture Jerusalem](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/d2/Taking_of_Jerusalem_by_the_Crusaders%2C_15th_July_1099.jpg)

## 1099-07-15

On July 15, 1099, Christian soldiers of the First Crusade successfully captured Jerusalem after a five-week siege. The city had been under Muslim control for centuries, and its fall marked a significant victory for the Crusaders. Following the breach of the city walls, a brutal massacre of most of the city's inhabitants, including Muslims, Jews, and some Eastern Christians, ensued. The capture established the Latin Kingdom of Jerusalem and had profound religious and political implications for both the Christian and Islamic worlds, initiating a complex period of conflict and coexistence in the Levant.
