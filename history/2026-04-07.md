# The World Health Organization Is Founded

![The World Health Organization Is Founded](https://data.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/WHO_logo.svg)

## 1948-04-07

On April 7, 1948, the World Health Organization (WHO) was officially established, with its constitution coming into force. This specialized agency of the United Nations was created to act as the coordinating authority on international public health matters. Its primary objective is the attainment by all peoples of the highest possible level of health. Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, the WHO plays a crucial role in disease prevention, health promotion, and setting international health standards, addressing global challenges such as infectious diseases and health emergencies.
