# Sinking of the RMS Titanic

![Sinking of the RMS Titanic](https://www.historic-uk.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/the-sinking-of-the-rms-titanic.jpg)

## 1912-04-15

The RMS Titanic, a British passenger liner, sank in the North Atlantic Ocean early on April 15, 1912, after striking an iceberg late the previous night. The ship was on its maiden voyage from Southampton to New York City with an estimated 2,224 passengers and crew aboard. More than 1,500 people perished, making it one of the deadliest maritime disasters in modern history. The tragedy led to widespread outrage over the lack of lifeboats and inadequate safety regulations, prompting significant improvements in maritime safety protocols internationally.
