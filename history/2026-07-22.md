# Wiley Post Completes First Solo Flight Around the World

![Wiley Post Completes First Solo Flight Around the World](https://sciencenotes.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Wiley_Post.jpg)

## 1933-07-22

On July 22, 1933, American aviator Wiley Post successfully completed the first solo flight around the world. Piloting his Lockheed Vega monoplane, the 'Winnie Mae,' Post landed at Floyd Bennett Field in New York City, marking the end of his ambitious journey. He covered approximately 15,596 miles in 7 days, 18 hours, and 49 minutes, a significant improvement over the record he set with navigator Harold Gatty in 1931. This remarkable feat demonstrated advancements in aviation technology and human endurance, solidifying Post's place as a pioneer in flight.
