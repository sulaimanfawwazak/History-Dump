# The Storming Of The Bastille

![The Storming Of The Bastille](https://cdn.britannica.com/98/90498-050-5527D0C1/prison-event-Bastille-French-Revolution-engraving-July-14-1789.jpg)

## 1789-07-14

On July 14, 1789, Parisian revolutionaries stormed the Bastille fortress-prison, a powerful symbol of royal tyranny in France. The large crowd sought gunpowder and arms, ultimately overwhelming the small garrison and its governor, Bernard-René de Launay, who was subsequently killed. Although only a handful of prisoners were held at the time, the fall of the Bastille represented a dramatic blow against the monarchy's absolute power. This pivotal event ignited the French Revolution, inspiring widespread uprisings and fundamentally altering the course of French and European history.
