# Watergate Scandal Begins

![Watergate Scandal Begins](https://cdn.britannica.com/15/164715-050-FC023F27/Pres-Charles-Wendell-Colson-Richard-M-Nixon.jpg)

## 1972-06-17

On June 17, 1972, five men were arrested for breaking into the Democratic National Committee headquarters at the Watergate complex in Washington D.C. This seemingly minor burglary ultimately unraveled into a major political scandal that engulfed the Nixon administration. Investigations revealed widespread abuses of power by the White House, including illegal wiretapping, obstruction of justice, and cover-ups. The scandal led to the resignation of President Richard Nixon in August 1974, the only U.S. president to do so.
