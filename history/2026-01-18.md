# Wilhelm I Proclaimed German Emperor

![Wilhelm I Proclaimed German Emperor](https://c8.alamy.com/comp/G39PKR/wilhelm-i-of-prussia-is-proclaimed-german-emperor-in-the-mirror-room-G39PKR.jpg)

## 1871-01-18

On January 18, 1871, Wilhelm I of Prussia was proclaimed German Emperor (Deutscher Kaiser) in the Hall of Mirrors at the Palace of Versailles, France. This momentous event marked the official unification of Germany under Prussian leadership, following its victory in the Franco-Prussian War. Otto von Bismarck, the Prussian Minister-President, played a pivotal role in orchestrating this unification and the subsequent establishment of the German Empire. The proclamation solidified Germany as a major European power, fundamentally altering the continent's political landscape and setting the stage for future conflicts.
