# Apple Computer Company Founded

![Apple Computer Company Founded](https://www.mercurynews.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/20160326__SJM-APPLEPLAY-0326-011.jpg?w=532)

## 1976-04-01

On April 1, 1976, Apple Computer Company was officially founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. Registered in Cupertino, California, this event marked the beginning of one of the most influential technology companies in the world. Their initial venture involved the Apple I computer, which Wozniak designed and built by hand. This foundational moment set the stage for Apple's future revolutionary products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, forever altering personal computing and mobile technology. Ronald Wayne famously sold his 10% stake in the company for $800 just 12 days after its formation.
