# Slovakia Declares Independence from Czechoslovakia

![Slovakia Declares Independence from Czechoslovakia](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a5/Prohl%C3%A1%C5%A1en%C3%AD_nez%C3%A1vislosti.jpg)

## 1939-03-14

On March 14, 1939, Slovakia declared its independence from Czechoslovakia, forming the First Slovak Republic. This move was orchestrated under intense pressure from Nazi Germany, with Adolf Hitler personally threatening Slovak leaders with a German invasion if they did not comply. Jozef Tiso became the leader of this new state, which effectively functioned as a client state of Germany throughout World War II. The declaration was a crucial step in Germany's dismemberment of Czechoslovakia, preceding the German occupation of the Czech lands the very next day.
