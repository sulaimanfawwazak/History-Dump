# Aleksei Leonov Performs First Spacewalk

![Aleksei Leonov Performs First Spacewalk](https://www.fai.org/sites/default/files/16616150859_9c1d6a55cd_o_1.jpg)

## 1965-03-18

On March 18, 1965, Soviet cosmonaut Aleksei Arkhipovich Leonov made history by becoming the first human to perform a spacewalk. Leonov exited his Voskhod 2 spacecraft for 12 minutes, floating freely tethered to the capsule while orbiting Earth. The groundbreaking feat demonstrated humanity's ability to operate outside a spacecraft in the vacuum of space. This monumental achievement was a significant Cold War victory for the Soviet Union in the space race, though Leonov faced extreme difficulties re-entering the spacecraft due to his suit expanding in the vacuum.
