# Super Bowl I Held In Los Angeles

![Super Bowl I Held In Los Angeles](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/c/c6/Super_Bowl_LVI_logo.png)

## 1967-01-15

On January 15, 1967, the first-ever AFL-NFL World Championship Game, now famously known as Super Bowl I, took place at the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. This historic contest pitted the NFL champion Green Bay Packers, led by legendary coach Vince Lombardi and quarterback Bart Starr, against the AFL champion Kansas City Chiefs. The Packers dominated the game, winning by a score of 35-10, thus establishing the NFL's supremacy at the time. This event marked the beginning of an enduring American sporting tradition and one of the world's most-watched annual athletic competitions.
