# Assassination of John F. Kennedy

![Assassination of John F. Kennedy](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/59/JFK_Motorcade_GettyImages-517330536.jpg)

## 1963-11-22

On November 22, 1963, John F. Kennedy, the 35th President of the United States, was assassinated in Dallas, Texas. He was riding in a motorcade with his wife, Jacqueline Kennedy, and Governor John Connally of Texas when shots were fired. Lee Harvey Oswald, a former Marine, was quickly identified and arrested as the primary suspect. The tragic event profoundly shocked the American nation and the world, sparking numerous investigations and conspiracy theories that continue to this day. His death marked a pivotal moment in American history, leading to Lyndon B. Johnson's immediate succession to the presidency.
