# The Assassination of Julius Caesar

![The Assassination of Julius Caesar](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/eb/Vincenzo_Camuccini_-_La_morte_di_Cesare.jpg)

## 0044-03-15

On March 15, 44 BC, Roman dictator Julius Caesar was assassinated by a group of senators, including Marcus Junius Brutus and Gaius Cassius Longinus. Fearing his growing power and ambition, the conspirators attacked Caesar at a meeting of the Senate in the Theatre of Pompey in Rome. He suffered 23 stab wounds and died at the base of a statue of his former rival, Pompey the Great. This dramatic event plunged the Roman Republic into a series of civil wars, ultimately leading to its demise and the rise of the Roman Empire under Octavian, Caesar's adopted son.
