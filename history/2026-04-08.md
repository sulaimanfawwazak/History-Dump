# Entente Cordiale Signed

![Entente Cordiale Signed](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/e/e5/Entente_cordiale_%28film%29.jpg)

## 1904-04-08

The Entente Cordiale, a series of agreements between the United Kingdom and France, was officially signed on April 8, 1904. This landmark diplomatic understanding resolved long-standing colonial disputes between the two powers, particularly concerning territories in Africa and elsewhere. While not a military alliance, it significantly improved Anglo-French relations and paved the way for closer cooperation in the years leading up to World War I. This agreement effectively ended centuries of intermittent conflict and suspicion, marking a new era of diplomatic alignment in Europe and reshaping the continent's political landscape.
