# The First Academy Awards Ceremony Held

![The First Academy Awards Ceremony Held](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/44/1st_Academy_Awards_Program_Cover.jpg/250px-1st_Academy_Awards_Program_Cover.jpg)

## 1929-05-16

The very first Academy Awards ceremony, officially known as the 1st Academy Awards, was held on May 16, 1929. This private dinner event took place in the Blossom Room of the Hollywood Roosevelt Hotel in Los Angeles, California, with approximately 270 people in attendance. The ceremony honored the outstanding film achievements from the 1927 and 1928 film seasons. Emil Jannings received the award for Best Actor, Janet Gaynor for Best Actress, and the film "Wings" was recognized as Outstanding Picture. This groundbreaking event laid the foundation for one of the most prestigious and enduring awards in the global entertainment industry.
