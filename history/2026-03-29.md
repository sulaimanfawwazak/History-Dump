# Last U.S. Combat Troops Leave Vietnam

![Last U.S. Combat Troops Leave Vietnam](https://www.zinnedproject.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/tdih_troops_vietnam.jpg)

## 1973-03-29

On March 29, 1973, the last U.S. combat troops departed South Vietnam, marking the official end of direct American military involvement in the Vietnam War. This withdrawal followed the signing of the Paris Peace Accords in January 1973, which aimed to establish peace and end the conflict. While U.S. combat operations ceased, a small number of military advisors and support personnel remained for a brief period. The departure concluded over a decade of significant American presence and fighting in Southeast Asia. However, the conflict between North and South Vietnam continued for two more years until the fall of Saigon.
