# The United States Purchases Alaska

![The United States Purchases Alaska](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/20/Alaska_Purchase_%28hi-res%29.jpg)

## 1867-03-30

On March 30, 1867, the United States finalized an agreement to purchase the territory of Alaska from the Russian Empire for a sum of $7.2 million. The historic treaty was negotiated by U.S. Secretary of State William Seward and Russian Minister to the United States Eduard de Stoeckl. This significant acquisition, encompassing approximately 586,412 square miles, effectively doubled the size of the United States at the time. While initially mocked by some critics as "Seward's Folly," the purchase later proved to be an immense strategic and economic success due to Alaska's vast natural resources and geopolitical importance. The formal transfer of sovereignty took place in October of the same year.
