# John Lennon Assassinated in New York City

![John Lennon Assassinated in New York City](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/e/ef/Police_artist_image_of_murder_of_John_Lennon.jpg)

## 1980-12-08

On December 8, 1980, legendary musician and former Beatles member John Lennon was tragically shot and killed outside his apartment building, The Dakota, in New York City. He was returning home with his wife, Yoko Ono, when Mark David Chapman, a disturbed fan, fired multiple shots at him. Lennon was rushed to Roosevelt Hospital but was pronounced dead shortly after his arrival. His untimely death sent shockwaves across the globe, sparking widespread grief and mourning, and marking a profound loss for the music world and popular culture.
