# The Battle of Berlin Begins

![The Battle of Berlin Begins](https://warfarehistorynetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spr19-Berlin-1-4C-scaled.jpg)

## 1945-04-16

On April 16, 1945, the Soviet Red Army launched its final major offensive of World War II's European theater, aimed at capturing Berlin, the capital of Nazi Germany. Led by Marshals Georgy Zhukov and Ivan Konev, millions of Soviet soldiers, supported by vast amounts of artillery and tanks, commenced their assault from positions along the Oder-Neisse line. This massive operation was designed to decisively defeat the last remnants of the Wehrmacht and bring an end to the war in Europe. The ensuing battle involved intense urban combat, leading to the eventual fall of the city and the collapse of the Nazi regime within weeks.
