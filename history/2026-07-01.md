# Canadian Confederation Established

![Canadian Confederation Established](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0a/Fathers_of_Confederation_LAC_c001855.jpg)

## 1867-07-01

On July 1, 1867, the British North America Act came into effect, officially establishing the Dominion of Canada. This landmark act united three British colonies—the Province of Canada (which was divided into Ontario and Quebec), Nova Scotia, and New Brunswick—into a single federal dominion. Sir John A. Macdonald assumed office as Canada's first Prime Minister, leading the new nation under a system of parliamentary democracy and constitutional monarchy. This event marked a crucial step in Canada's journey towards self-governance and laid the foundation for its development into a modern, independent country. The date is now celebrated annually as Canada Day, commemorating the nation's founding.
