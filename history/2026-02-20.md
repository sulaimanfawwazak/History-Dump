# John Glenn Orbits Earth

![John Glenn Orbits Earth](https://earthsky.org/upl/2014/02/john-glenn-friendship-7.jpg)

## 1962-02-20

On February 20, 1962, American astronaut John Glenn became the first American to orbit the Earth, doing so aboard the Mercury-Atlas 6 spacecraft named "Friendship 7." His historic flight completed three orbits around the planet in just under five hours, reaching an altitude of approximately 162 miles. This achievement marked a crucial milestone for the United States in the Space Race against the Soviet Union, boosting national morale and demonstrating America's growing capabilities in human spaceflight. Glenn's successful mission solidified his place as an iconic figure in space exploration and paved the way for future manned missions, including the Apollo program.
