# The Schuman Declaration Proposes European Coal and Steel Community

![The Schuman Declaration Proposes European Coal and Steel Community](https://lookaside.fbsbx.com/lookaside/crawler/media/?media_id=1328994162665965)

## 1950-05-09

On May 9, 1950, French Foreign Minister Robert Schuman delivered a declaration proposing the creation of a European Coal and Steel Community (ECSC). This groundbreaking proposal, often regarded as the genesis of the European Union, aimed to place French and West German coal and steel production under a common high authority. Schuman envisioned that pooling these vital resources would make war between France and Germany "not merely unthinkable, but materially impossible." The declaration led to the Treaty of Paris in 1951, establishing the ECSC, which eventually evolved into the EU, promoting peace and economic integration in Europe.
