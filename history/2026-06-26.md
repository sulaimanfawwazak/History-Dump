# John F. Kennedy Delivers "Ich Bin Ein Berliner" Speech

![John F. Kennedy Delivers "Ich Bin Ein Berliner" Speech](https://lookaside.fbsbx.com/lookaside/crawler/media/?media_id=875672337925756)

## 1963-06-26

On June 26, 1963, U.S. President John F. Kennedy delivered his iconic "Ich bin ein Berliner" speech in West Berlin. Standing before a crowd of hundreds of thousands at the Rudolph Wilde Platz (now John-F.-Kennedy-Platz), Kennedy passionately declared American support for West Germany and its people amidst the Cold War. His declaration, "All free men, wherever they may live, are citizens of Berlin, and therefore, as a free man, I take pride in the words 'Ich bin ein Berliner!'" symbolized solidarity against communist oppression. The speech is widely regarded as one of his most powerful anti-communist statements and a pivotal moment in Cold War diplomacy, boosting the morale of West Berliners living under the shadow of the Berlin Wall.
