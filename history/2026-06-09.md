## 1934-06-09

On June 9, 1934, the beloved animated character Donald Duck made his first official appearance in Walt Disney's Silly Symphonies cartoon, 'The Wise Little Hen.' This short film saw Donald, along with Peter Pig, attempt to avoid helping Mrs. Hen plant and harvest her corn, feigning illness with humorous antics. Though a supporting character in this debut, Donald's distinctive voice, provided by Clarence Nash, and his feisty, often short-tempered personality quickly resonated with audiences. This initial appearance paved the way for Donald to become one of Disney's most iconic and enduring characters, eventually starring in his own series of cartoons and features.
