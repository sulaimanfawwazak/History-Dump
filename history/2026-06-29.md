# Original iPhone Released

![Original iPhone Released](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2024/03/27/iphone-1711569354625.png)

## 2007-06-29

On June 29, 2007, Apple Inc. released its first generation iPhone in the United States. Unveiled by CEO Steve Jobs in January of that year, the device combined a mobile phone, an iPod, and an internet communications device into one sleek multi-touch interface. Its launch marked a pivotal moment in technology, fundamentally changing the mobile phone industry and paving the way for the smartphone era. The iPhone's intuitive design and innovative features quickly gained immense popularity, establishing Apple as a dominant force in the telecommunications market.
