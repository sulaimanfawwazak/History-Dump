# Austria-Hungary Issues Ultimatum To Serbia

![Austria-Hungary Issues Ultimatum To Serbia](https://www.mrallsophistory.com/revision/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/07-23a.png)

## 1914-07-23

On July 23, 1914, Austria-Hungary delivered an ultimatum to Serbia following the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand by a Serbian nationalist in Sarajevo a month earlier. The ten-point ultimatum demanded that Serbia suppress all anti-Austrian propaganda and allow Austrian officials to participate in investigations and judicial proceedings within Serbia related to the assassination plot. Serbia accepted eight of the ten demands but refused the two that it deemed infringed upon its sovereignty. This partial refusal provided Austria-Hungary with the pretext it sought, leading to its declaration of war on Serbia five days later, which rapidly escalated into the First World War.
