# First Barbie Doll Introduced to the World

![First Barbie Doll Introduced to the World](https://www.nydailynews.com/wp-content/uploads/migration/2023/03/08/OBMC3N45CSQ53JR5HLHGIFMIAE.jpg)

## 1959-03-09

On March 9, 1959, the first Barbie doll, developed by Mattel co-founder Ruth Handler, made its official debut at the American International Toy Fair in New York City. Handler had conceived the idea after observing her daughter, Barbara (for whom the doll was named), play with paper dolls and noting the market's lack of three-dimensional adult-figured dolls. Barbie quickly became a sensation, offering children a doll with a sophisticated, fashionable aesthetic unlike any on the market at the time. This groundbreaking toy revolutionized the doll industry, growing into an iconic global brand that has influenced generations. Her introduction marked a significant moment in cultural history and toy manufacturing.
