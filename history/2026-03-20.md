# Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack

![Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack](https://www.cwccoalition.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/unnamed-1024x747.jpg)

## 1995-03-20

On March 20, 1995, members of the Aum Shinrikyo cult carried out a coordinated sarin nerve gas attack on the Tokyo subway system during rush hour. The attackers simultaneously released sarin on five different trains across three major lines, affecting multiple stations. This act of domestic terrorism resulted in 13 deaths, over 5,000 people suffering injuries to varying degrees, and widespread panic across the city. The incident profoundly shocked Japan and led to a major crackdown on the cult and reevaluation of national security measures.
