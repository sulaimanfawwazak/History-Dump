# Adolf Eichmann Captured In Argentina

![Adolf Eichmann Captured In Argentina](https://wwv.yadvashem.org/yv/en/exhibitions/eichmann/images/capturing/01.jpg)

## 1960-05-11

On May 11, 1960, Israeli Mossad agents successfully captured Adolf Eichmann in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Eichmann, a former Nazi SS-Obersturmbannführer, had been a principal organizer of the Holocaust, responsible for facilitating the mass deportation of Jews to extermination camps. After World War II, he fled Germany and lived under various aliases, evading justice for over a decade. His capture was a culmination of years of intelligence gathering and a covert operation, bringing one of the most sought-after Nazi war criminals to justice. This event allowed for his subsequent trial in Israel, where he was convicted of war crimes and crimes against humanity.
