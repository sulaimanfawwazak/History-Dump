# Tiananmen Square Massacre

![Tiananmen Square Massacre](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/d/dd/Tank_Man_%28Tiananmen_Square_protester%29.jpg)

## 1989-06-04

On June 4, 1989, the Chinese government launched a violent crackdown on student-led pro-democracy protests centered in Tiananmen Square, Beijing. For weeks, thousands of students, intellectuals, and workers had gathered, demanding political reforms and an end to corruption. Troops and tanks were deployed, opening fire on demonstrators and civilians attempting to block their advance. The exact death toll remains unknown and disputed, but thousands are believed to have been killed or injured. This brutal suppression solidified the Communist Party's authoritarian control and drew widespread international condemnation.
