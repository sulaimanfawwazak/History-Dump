# Founding of Jamestown Colony

![Founding of Jamestown Colony](https://www.nps.gov/jame/learn/historyculture/images/D-4-Colonists-Landing-at-Jamestown-for-web.jpg?maxwidth=650&autorotate=false)

## 1607-05-13

On May 13, 1607, a group of approximately 100 English settlers, sponsored by the Virginia Company of London, landed on a peninsula in the James River in present-day Virginia. They established Jamestown, the first permanent English settlement in North America. Led by figures like Captain John Smith, the colonists faced immense hardships, including disease, famine, and conflict with local Native American tribes, particularly the Powhatan Confederacy. Despite early struggles and near-failure, Jamestown ultimately survived and laid the groundwork for future English colonization and the eventual formation of the United States.
