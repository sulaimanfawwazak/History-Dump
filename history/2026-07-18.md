# Nelson Mandela Is Born

![Nelson Mandela Is Born](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/02/Nelson_Mandela_1994.jpg)

## 1918-07-18

On July 18, 1918, Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela was born in Mvezo, Cape Province, Union of South Africa. He would later become a pivotal figure in the anti-apartheid movement, fighting against institutionalized racial segregation and discrimination. Mandela endured 27 years in prison for his activism, becoming an international symbol of resistance and hope. Upon his release, he played a crucial role in negotiating an end to apartheid and was subsequently elected the first president of a democratic South Africa in 1994. His legacy continues to inspire movements for human rights and equality worldwide.
