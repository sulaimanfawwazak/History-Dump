# The Liberty Bell Rings for Independence

![The Liberty Bell Rings for Independence](https://www.nps.gov/inde/images/DSC8249.jpg)

## 1776-07-08

On July 8, 1776, the Liberty Bell, then known as the State House Bell, was rung in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to gather citizens for the first public reading of the Declaration of Independence. Colonel John Nixon read the newly adopted document aloud to the gathered crowd in the State House yard. This historic moment marked the official public announcement of the thirteen American colonies' formal separation from Great Britain. The bell's inscription, "Proclaim Liberty throughout all the Land unto all the Inhabitants thereof," from Leviticus 25:10, became especially symbolic on this momentous occasion.
