# Deaths of John Adams And Thomas Jefferson

![Deaths of John Adams And Thomas Jefferson](https://historicipswich.net/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/9d79a-adams-jefferson-fourth-july-1.jpg?w=718)

## 1826-07-04

On July 4, 1826, exactly 50 years after the adoption of the Declaration of Independence, two of its most prominent signers and former U.S. Presidents, John Adams and Thomas Jefferson, died within hours of each other. Adams passed away in Quincy, Massachusetts, while Jefferson died at Monticello, Virginia. Their deaths on the nation's semicentennial were seen as a profound sign by many Americans, symbolizing the passing of the revolutionary generation. Both men had a complex relationship, from allies to rivals and eventually reconciled through correspondence in their later years.
