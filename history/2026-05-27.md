# Sinking of the German Battleship Bismarck

![Sinking of the German Battleship Bismarck](https://collections.rmg.co.uk/media/381/164/bhc0679.jpg)

## 1941-05-27

On May 27, 1941, the German battleship Bismarck, one of the most formidable warships of World War II, was sunk in the North Atlantic by the British Royal Navy. The culmination of a relentless hunt, the Bismarck had previously sunk the British battlecruiser HMS Hood just three days prior. After being crippled by a torpedo from a British Fairey Swordfish biplane, which jammed its rudder, the unmaneuverable ship was then relentlessly shelled by battleships HMS Rodney and HMS King George V. Its destruction was a major victory for the Allies, eliminating a significant threat to transatlantic shipping convoys and boosting British morale during the Battle of the Atlantic.
