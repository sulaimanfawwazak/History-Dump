# First Human Heart Transplant Performed

![First Human Heart Transplant Performed](https://www.news.uct.ac.za/images/userfiles/files/gallery/20171129_Heart50/01.jpg)

## 1967-12-03

On December 3, 1967, Dr. Christiaan Barnard successfully performed the world's first human-to-human heart transplant at Groote Schuur Hospital in Cape Town, South Africa. The pioneering surgery involved transplanting the heart of Denise Darvall, a 25-year-old car crash victim, into 53-year-old grocer Louis Washkansky. While Washkansky survived the operation, he tragically succumbed to pneumonia eighteen days later due to his suppressed immune system. This groundbreaking medical achievement captivated the world and marked a monumental leap forward in the field of organ transplantation, opening new possibilities for treating severe heart disease. It ignited intense ethical and scientific debates but ultimately paved the way for subsequent advancements in surgical techniques and immunosuppressant drugs.
