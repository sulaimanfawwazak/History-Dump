# President Truman Signs Marshall Plan Into Law

![President Truman Signs Marshall Plan Into Law](https://www.atlanticcouncil.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/marhall-foundation-4-3-12-marshall_plan_signing.jpg)

## 1948-04-03

On April 3, 1948, U.S. President Harry S. Truman signed the Foreign Assistance Act, better known as the Marshall Plan, into law. This monumental post-World War II initiative provided over $13 billion (equivalent to over $150 billion in today's currency) in economic aid to help rebuild war-torn Western European economies. Officially known as the European Recovery Program, it aimed to prevent the spread of communism by fostering stability and prosperity, countering Soviet influence in the continent. The plan significantly contributed to Europe's rapid economic recovery and strengthened political ties with the United States.
