# First Telephone Call Made

![First Telephone Call Made](https://whyy.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/MartinCooper1200-768x509.jpg)

## 1876-03-10

On March 10, 1876, Alexander Graham Bell successfully made the first intelligible telephone call to his assistant, Thomas A. Watson. The historic call took place in Boston, Massachusetts, with Bell speaking the famous words, "Mr. Watson, come here, I want to see you." Watson, in a nearby room, clearly heard Bell's voice through the device. This monumental achievement marked a pivotal moment in communication history, demonstrating the practical application of Bell's patented invention. It laid the groundwork for the global telecommunications networks that would profoundly transform society, business, and personal interactions for centuries to come.
