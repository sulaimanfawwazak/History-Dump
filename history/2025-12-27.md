## 1831-12-27

On December 27, 1831, the HMS Beagle departed from Plymouth, England, initiating a momentous five-year scientific survey voyage around the world. Aboard was the young naturalist Charles Darwin, whose keen observations during this journey would lay the groundwork for his revolutionary theories on evolution.
