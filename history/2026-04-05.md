# Winston Churchill Resigns As Prime Minister

![Winston Churchill Resigns As Prime Minister](https://static.politico.com/98/1f/d763372846e788b7d0956fb2d311/190404-winston-churchill-ap-773.jpg)

## 1955-04-05

On April 5, 1955, Sir Winston Churchill, a towering figure in 20th-century British and world politics, resigned as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom at the age of 80. Citing ill health, Churchill stepped down after a second term, during which he focused on consolidating post-war recovery and maintaining Britain's international standing. His resignation marked the end of an extraordinary political career that spanned over 50 years, including his pivotal leadership during World War II. He was succeeded by his long-time ally and Foreign Secretary, Anthony Eden, ushering in a new era for the Conservative Party.
