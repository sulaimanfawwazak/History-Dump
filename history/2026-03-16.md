# My Lai Massacre

![My Lai Massacre](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/77/My_Lai_massacre.jpg)

## 1968-03-16

On March 16, 1968, during the Vietnam War, American soldiers from Company C, 1st Battalion, 20th Infantry Regiment, committed a horrific massacre of unarmed Vietnamese civilians in the hamlet of My Lai, Sơn Mỹ, Quảng Ngãi Province. Hundreds of villagers, mostly women, children, and elderly men, were systematically killed in an act of profound brutality and disregard for human life. The incident, initially covered up, came to light over a year later, sparking international outrage and significantly turning public opinion against the war in the United States. Only one officer, Lieutenant William Calley, was ever convicted for his role in the massacre. This event remains one of the darkest chapters in U.S. military history.
