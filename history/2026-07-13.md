# Live Aid Concerts Held Globally

![Live Aid Concerts Held Globally](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/9/9c/Official_Live_Aid_poster.jpeg)

## 1985-07-13

On July 13, 1985, two massive "Live Aid" concerts took place simultaneously at Wembley Stadium in London, England, and John F. Kennedy Stadium in Philadelphia, United States. Organized by Bob Geldof and Midge Ure, the event aimed to raise funds for famine relief in Ethiopia. Featuring an unprecedented lineup of rock and pop superstars, including Queen, David Bowie, U2, Madonna, and Paul McCartney, the concerts were broadcast worldwide to an estimated 1.9 billion viewers in 150 countries. Live Aid successfully raised over $127 million for the cause, becoming a landmark event in both music and humanitarian history and demonstrating the power of global media for charitable endeavors.
