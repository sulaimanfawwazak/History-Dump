# Yellowstone National Park Established

![Yellowstone National Park Established](https://www.nps.gov/yell/learn/historyculture/images/Moran-Blue.jpg)

## 1872-03-01

On March 1, 1872, U.S. President Ulysses S. Grant signed the Yellowstone National Park Protection Act into law. This landmark legislation designated a vast area in the Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho territories as the world's first national park, "dedicated and set apart as a public park or pleasuring-ground for the benefit and enjoyment of the people." The establishment of Yellowstone marked a pivotal moment in the global conservation movement, preserving its unique geothermal features, abundant wildlife, and stunning natural beauty for future generations. This act set a crucial precedent for the creation of national parks worldwide. It underscored a growing international recognition of the importance of protecting significant natural landscapes.
